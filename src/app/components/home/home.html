Ticket to Riot

RVA's Own Punk Rock Beatles Tribute Band

About Us

Ticket to Riot brings you the Fab Four's greatest hits reimagined through the raw, energetic lens of punk rock. Based in Richmond, VA, we smash the boundaries between Beatlemania and the spirit of '77.

From "Anarchy in the U.K." versions of Lennon-McCartney classics to stripped-down, three-chord blasts of pure Beatles brilliance — we are your ticket to riot.

What We Offer

  • Punk-fuelled Beatles covers
  • Live shows across RVA and beyond
  • Exclusive band merchandise
  • Private events & venue bookings
